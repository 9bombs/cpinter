Application Guidelines & Checklist


Application form submission :

·         Applicant is required to submit both hard copy and soft copy of the application form and all required documents below :

[ ]  A CU application form & Checklist (this page)

[ ]  An official transcript
[ ]  A Degree Certificate

[ ]  A statement of purpose*

[ ]  An English proficiency score record

[ ]  Academic references** (letters of recommendation) in sealed and signed envelopes

[ ]  A photocopy of published papers (for application to Ph.D. program)

[ ]  Additional documents required by the department (if any, pls.refer to Dep.website attached)

 

* Statement of Purpose : Use a separate sheet to write about your motivation, objective of study, research experience and publications (if any), field of interest and research area which you would like to conduct at Chulalongkorn University, and expected benefits and plan after graduation. 

** Two references are required for the application to the Master’s Degree program. Three references are required for the application to the Ph.D. program.

·         Hard copy sort the document according to list above and submit to :

Case1 :  Financial Source = AUN/SEED-Net Scholarship

                Submit to the coordinator at the sending institution.

Case2 : Other Financial Source

                Submit to the Following address :

          International School of Engineering

          Engineering 2 Bldg.

          Faculty of Engineering, Chulalongkorn University

254  ayathai Road, Pathumwan, Bangkok 10330, Thailand       

 

·         Soft copy (scanned version of application form + all required documents) should be emailed to  int.grad.ise@gmail.com (please put  “SEM/ACADEMIC YEAR-GRAD_APP:YOUR NAME as the title of email e.g. 2/2015-GRAD_APP:PETER WONG)

·        Application Timeline :

Case1 :  Financial Source = AUN/SEED-Net Scholarship

        Hard copy of application submitted within Dec 31 will be considered for enrollment of preferred semester in the  next academic year.  E.g. If prefer academic year is 2015 (any semester), the application should be submitted before Dec 31, 2014.        

Case2 : Other Financial Source

        Soft copy of application should be emailed to int.grad.ise@gmail.com at least 2 months prior to the semester start date. Otherwise, your application will be considered for enrollment in the subsequent semester.
        Nonetheless, if you want to apply for certain scholarship, please respect the deadline for each scholarship that is specified from the scholarship website.

If there is any inquiry, feel free to contact us at
